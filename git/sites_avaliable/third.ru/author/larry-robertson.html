Larry RobertsonThe Innovator's Edge

Larry Robertson is an innovation advisor who works, writes, and guides at the nexus of creativity, leadership, and entrepreneurship. Named a Fulbright Scholar in 2021, a rarity for non-academic professionals, he is also the author of two award-winning books: The Language of Man. Learning to Speak Creativity and A Deliberate Pause: Entrepreneurship and Its Moment in Human Progress. As founder of Lighthouse Consulting, he has for three decades guided entrepreneurial ventures and their leaders through growth to lasting success. His third book, Rebel Leadership: How to Thrive in Uncertain Times, will be released June 1, 2021. Learn more here.
