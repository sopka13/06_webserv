Paul SchoemakerThe Strategic Decision

Paul J. H. Schoemaker, PhD is an author, speaker, and academic (Univ of Chicago and Wharton). He founded Decision Strategies International where he served for decades as CEO and Chairman for 25 years; he recently sold this company and founded Q2 Tech, a digital tools venture. Schoemaker is the former research director of the Mack Institute for Innovation Management at the Wharton School, where he taught strategy and decision making for many years. He has published 11 books and well over 100 articles; his most recent book is See Sooner--Act Faster: How Vigilant Leaders Thrive in an Era of Digital Turbulence with George Day (MIT Press, 2019).
