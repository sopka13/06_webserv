Maria HaggertyFinding Fulfillment

Maria Haggerty is CEO of Dotcom Distribution, which provides B2C and B2B fulfillment and logistic services to emerging and established e-commerce brands. Since co-founding Dotcom Distribution in 1999, she has played a critical role in developing and defining all aspects of the business, including sales and marketing, operations, finance and IT. She practices the company’s core value pillars (excitement, trust, and opportunity) through her innovative mindset and hands-on leadership style. Prior to Dotcom, Maria began her career as a CPA at Arthur Andersen and was the CFO of GoodTimes Home Video.
