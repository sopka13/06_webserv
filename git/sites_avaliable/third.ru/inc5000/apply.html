          Frequently Asked Questions

          • Is my company eligible?
            All of the following must be true of your company in order to qualify for the 2021 Inc. 5000 Regionals. Your company must:
            1. Have generated revenue by March 31, 2017
            2. Have generated at least $100,000 in revenue in 2017
            3. Have generated at least $2 million in revenue in 2020
            4. Be privately held, for profit; based in the U.S., and independent (not a subsidiary or division of another company).
          • Why should my company apply?
            1. Feature In Inc. magazine: The top 500 companies will be featured in Inc. magazine and all 5000 companies will be featured on Inc.com.
            2. Dedicated Company Profile on Inc.com: Every company that makes the list will receive a dedicated company profile on Inc.com. The Inc. 5000 company profiles receive millions of impressions ever year.
            3. Worldwide Spotlight: The elite Inc. 5000 list gives your company greater credibility with clients, investors, and the media often leading to meaningful connections to grow your business.
            4. Team Recognition: This award publicly acknowledges your team's hard work and can help you recruit new employees.
          • What are my odds of making the list?
            The Inc. 5000 only looks at revenue for two years, 2020 and 2017. If your company made more gross revenue in 2020 then it did in 2017 than your odds of making the list are very good!
          • What’s the application process?
            Pretty simple! You’ll fill out the application which should only take a few minutes. Then we’ll ask you to verify your 2020 and 2017 revenue figures. That’s it.
