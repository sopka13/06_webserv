Dr. Bret Fund

Dr. Bret Fund is the Head of Cybersecurity and Vice President of Education at Flatiron School, a global institution that trains students in 21st-century skills like cybersecurity, software engineering, data science, and product design. Prior to Flatiron School, Bret founded and served as CEO of SecureSet, a tech academy focused on educating the next generation of cybersecurity professionals. Bret holds a PhD in Business Administration from Pennsylvania State University and is a former professor at the University of Colorado - Boulder.
