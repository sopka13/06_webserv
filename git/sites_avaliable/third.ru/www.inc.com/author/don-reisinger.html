Don ReisingerThe Intersection of Tech and Business

Don Reisinger is one of the leading technology and business writers in the world with bylines in publications as far-reaching as CNET, Fortune Magazine, Fortune.com, TIME, The Los Angeles Times, The New York Times, TechCrunch, Tom’s Guide, PCMag, and several others. Don started his freelance writing career with PCMag’s Gearlog blog, where he wrote about gadgetry. He quickly moved on to CNET and spent several years there writing about everything from gadgetry to the “digital home” to startups. Don has written on a variety of topics in his career, including big-name companies, gadgets, video games, enterprise topics, and everything in between.
